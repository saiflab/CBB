About Me

I am currently pursuing my Master's in Pharmacy at the University of Rajshahi, where I also completed my undergraduate studies. My academic journey has instilled in me a rigorous scientific foundation, which I am now applying to the cutting-edge fields of Bioinformatics and Artificial Intelligence.

My goal is to harness computational power to advance healthcare systems and discover novel therapeutics that can alleviate human suffering. This drive has led me to actively engage in applied research, working as a Research Assistant in multiple reputable laboratories.

Beyond the lab, I am an avid traveler, movie enthusiast, and volunteer. I believe in a holistic approach to life—balancing rigorous scientific inquiry with mindfulness and exploration.
